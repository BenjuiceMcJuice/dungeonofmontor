## Design Decisions
- WET/CHARGED as catalyst conditions creates a whole new layer of tactical combo play — throw water then electrify, or freeze a wet enemy instantly
- Enemy AI behaviours make each enemy type feel distinct — slugs coat in slime, rats flee and drop bags, bigger enemies howl or sacrifice
- SEPSIS rework (30% per-tick) is much fairer than permanent damage mutation while still being threatening
- Tap-gated condition flow gives the player full control over pacing — no more missing important info due to timers
- Traps guarding valuable loot creates a genuine risk/reward decision — getting hit by a trap is a consolation prize, not just punishment
- Dice relics add a roguelike gambling layer that makes each run feel different
- Timed bombs add urgency and planning — do you throw it now or wait for the perfect moment?
